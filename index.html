The Global Job Market & AI

Technology is the world's fastest-growing industry. See why choosing a computing qualification secures your future.

AI Upgrades Jobs

"Will AI take my job?" No. AI automates boring tasks. The workforce desperately needs humans to do the creative problem-solving, build the AI models, and design interfaces that make AI easy for the public to use.

Work Anywhere

Tech skills are universal. A qualification in CS or DIT opens global doors. You can travel abroad, or sit comfortably in the UK and work entirely remotely for massive international companies in the US, Europe, or Asia.

High Earning Potential

Due to the massive global shortage of skilled tech workers, starting salaries are significantly higher than average. You can enter the industry via University, or take a highly-paid Degree Apprenticeship right after school.
